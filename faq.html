Frequently Asked Questions

What is Spurr?

Do you want to be more active but lack the motivation? Spurr makes it more fun by creating a common goal for a whole team. For example, you can create a challenge to do 1,000 pushups in 7 days together. Get statistics and count everyone's contributions towards your common goal.

How can start using Spurr?

Spurr is a Slack app, so the only way to use it is to connect it to your Slack workspace. You can do so directly on spurrapp.com by clicking on the 'Add to Slack' button.

Is Spurr free?

Yes, you can create challenges with up to 8 participants completely for free.

For bigger teams there currently are 2 paid plans: $19/mo ($189/y) for challenges up to 20 participants and $39/mo ($380/y) for challenges up to 40 participants. If you have specific needs we offer also enterprise plan. For more information please email hello@spurrapp.com.

What kind of challenges can I do?

That's completely up to you, but here are some ideas:

  1. Walk 1,000,000 steps together in a week.
  2. Do 1,000 push ups in 10 days.
  3. Do 5,000 squats in 10 days.
  4. 100 cigarette-free days.
  5. 50 coffee-free days.
  6. Read 5,000 pages in two weeks.
  7. Run 500 km in a month.

The goal depends on your team size, but it should always be challenging. We recommend creating shorter challenges (1-2 weeks long) as they tend to be more engaging – but it's entirely up to you and your team.
