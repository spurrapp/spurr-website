Frequently Asked Questions

What is Spurr?

Do you want to be more active but lack the motivation? Spurr will motivate your team to be more active while making it really fun. You choose a goal in points (e.g. 10,000 points) and a deadline to get to the goal. Each member of the team can then earn points for the whole team by doing different activities (e.g. do a few push-ups, go for a quick run,…).

How can I start using Spurr?

Spurr is a Slack app, so the only way to use it is to connect it to your Slack workspace. You can do so directly on spurrapp.com by clicking on the 'Add to Slack' button.

Is Spurr free?

Yes, you can create as many challenges as you want with up to 10 participants completely for free.

For bigger teams there currently are 2 paid plans:

  • 20 participants, 1 concurrent challenge for $19/month. ($0.95 / participant)
  • 40 participants, 2 concurrent challenges for $39/month. ($0.48 / participant)
If you have specific needs we offer also enterprise plan. For more information please email hello@spurrapp.com.

What kind of challenges can I do?

There are 2 types of challenges. You can either define how many points you want to reach and people can earn points by doing their favorite activity. Or you can create a single activity challenge that allows only one type of activity (e.g. running, steps counting, push-ups,…)

The goal depends on your team size, but it should always be challenging. We recommend creating shorter challenges (1-2 weeks long) as they tend to be more engaging – but it's entirely up to you and your team.

What if I want a challenge tailored to my team needs?

While Spurr is very flexible in the types of challenges you can do, you might find that you are missing a specific feature that would be valuable to your team. If that's the case please reach out to us at hello@spurrapp.com and we will be happy to help if we can.
